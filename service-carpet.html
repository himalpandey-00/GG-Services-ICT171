Carpet Cleaning

About This Service

Our carpet cleaning service uses industry-standard hot-water extraction equipment to deep clean carpets, removing embedded soil, pet dander, bacteria, and allergens. We also offer dry-cleaning for delicate carpets. Carpets are typically dry within 2–4 hours.

What's Included

  • Hot-water extraction (steam cleaning)
  • Dry carpet cleaning for sensitive fibres
  • Pre-treatment of stains and high-traffic areas
  • Deodorising treatment available
  • Scotchgard stain protection available
  • Suitable for residential and commercial carpets
  • Area rugs cleaned on-site or off-site
